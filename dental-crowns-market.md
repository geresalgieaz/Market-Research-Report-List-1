Materials used for crowns include porcelain, ceramic, metal, and resin, allowing for versatility based on patient needs.</p><p>The Dental Crowns Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period, driven by an aging population, increasing prevalence of dental issues, and rising awareness about oral health. <p><p>The dental crowns market features several types, including porcelain-fused-to-metal (PFM), all-ceramics, and metal crowns. PFM crowns combine the strength of metal with the aesthetic appeal of porcelain, making them suitable for both front and back teeth. All-ceramic crowns offer superior aesthetics and are often used for front teeth due to their natural appearance and biocompatibility. Metal crowns, typically made from gold or other alloys, are highly durable and are often used in molars where strength is essential.</p></p>
