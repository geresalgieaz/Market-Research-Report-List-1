<p><p>Precision viticulture encompasses three key market types: Guidance Systems, Remote Sensing, and Variable-Rate Technology. Guidance Systems utilize GPS and automated steering to enhance vineyard operations, improving task efficiency and accuracy. Remote Sensing involves using satellite imagery and drones to monitor vineyard health, allowing for real-time assessment of crop conditions. Variable-Rate Technology enables tailored application of inputs like water and fertilizers based on specific vineyard zones, optimizing resource use and improving overall crop yield and quality.</p></p>
