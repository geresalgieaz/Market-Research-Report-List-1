In contrast, two-arm surgical lights have dual articulated arms, providing enhanced positioning and coverage, ideal for larger surgical environments. Both types ensure optimal illumination of the surgical field, enhancing visibility and precision during procedures while accommodating varying spatial requirements and surgical needs.</p></p>
