<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 3D EP Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Mapping Navigation System</li><li>Contact Mapping Navigation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D EP Navigation Systems market encompasses two primary types: Non-Contact Mapping Navigation Systems and Contact Mapping Navigation Systems. Non-Contact Systems utilize electromagnetic fields or advanced imaging techniques to create maps of the electrical activity in the heart without physical contact, offering enhanced patient comfort and reduced risk of complications. In contrast, Contact Mapping Systems employ catheters that come into direct contact with the heart tissue, providing high-resolution data crucial for accurate diagnoses and targeted therapies in electrophysiological procedures.</p></p>
