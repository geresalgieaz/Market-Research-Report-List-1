<p><p>The dust-free paper market consists of three main types: Latex-bonded Airlaid Paper, Thermal-bonded Airlaid Paper, and Multi-bonded Airlaid Paper. Latex-bonded Airlaid Paper utilizes latex adhesives for superior durability and softness, making it ideal for hygiene products. Thermal-bonded Airlaid Paper employs heat for bonding, resulting in a lightweight and absorbent material, often used in medical and hygiene applications. Multi-bonded Airlaid Paper combines various bonding techniques to enhance performance, offering versatility in usage across diverse industries, such as personal care and household products.</p></p>
