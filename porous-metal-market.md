<p><p>The porous metal market is categorized based on porosity levels, which influence applications and properties. Low porosity materials (below 30%) offer high strength and lower permeability, ideal for structural components. Medium porosity (30-60%) balances strength and permeability, making them suitable for filtration and lightweight structures. High porosity materials (above 60%) are characterized by significant openness, allowing for applications in biomedical fields and catalysis, where enhanced surface area and fluid flow are essential. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
