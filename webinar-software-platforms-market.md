<p><p>Webinar software platforms can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on an organization's servers, offering more control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based platforms are hosted online, providing easy access, scalability, and lower initial costs. They enable users to conduct webinars from anywhere with an internet connection, making them more flexible and cost-effective for many businesses seeking to reach wider audiences.</p></p>
