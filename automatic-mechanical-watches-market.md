<p><strong>The Automatic Mechanical Watches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand Chain Mechanical Watch</li><li>Automatic Chain - up Hand Mechanical Watch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic mechanical watches market consists of two primary types: hand chain mechanical watches and automatic chain-up hand mechanical watches. Hand chain mechanical watches require manual winding by the wearer to operate, showcasing traditional craftsmanship. In contrast, automatic chain-up hand mechanical watches utilize a rotor mechanism that winds the mainspring through the motion of the wearer's wrist, providing a self-sustaining power source. Both types offer unique features that appeal to different segments of watch enthusiasts and collectors.</p></p>
