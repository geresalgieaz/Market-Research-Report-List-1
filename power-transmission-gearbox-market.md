<p><p>The power transmission gearbox market consists of various types, each serving distinct applications. Helical gearboxes feature angled teeth for smooth and efficient power transfer, ideal for high-load applications. Planetary gearboxes use multiple gears for compact design and high torque output, often found in automotive and industrial machinery. Bevel gearboxes enable perpendicular shaft orientation, suitable for applications requiring directional changes. Other types may include worm, spur, and hypoid gearboxes, each tailored to specific operational needs and performance criteria.</p></p>
