<p><p>Machine vision cameras are essential in the industrial market for automating inspection and quality control processes. Area scan cameras capture a two-dimensional image, ideal for stationary applications like monitoring products on a conveyor belt. They provide high-resolution images for a wide field of view. Line scan cameras, on the other hand, capture one line of pixels at a time, making them suitable for high-speed applications such as inspecting long or continuous materials, like paper or textiles, where movement is paramount.</p></p>
