<p><p>The disposable menstrual hygiene products market is categorized into three main types: menstrual pads, pantyliners, and tampons. Menstrual pads are absorbent items worn externally on underwear to capture menstrual flow, available in various sizes and absorbencies. Pantyliners are thinner and often used for daily protection or light flow. Tampons, designed to be inserted into the vagina, absorb menstrual fluid internally and come in different absorbency levels. Each product caters to specific preferences and needs of menstruating individuals.</p></p>
