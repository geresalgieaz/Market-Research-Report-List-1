<p><p>Intelligent aquaculture encompasses two primary market types: open systems and closed systems. Open systems involve natural water bodies, relying on environmental conditions for fish farming, which can lead to challenges like disease and pollution. In contrast, closed systems utilize controlled environments, often re-circulating water and optimizing conditions for growth, ensuring greater biosecurity and efficient resource use. Both systems apply advanced technologies to enhance sustainability and productivity while addressing environmental concerns in aquaculture practices.</p></p>
