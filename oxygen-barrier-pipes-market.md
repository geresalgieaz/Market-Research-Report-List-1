<p><ul><li>PE-RT Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li><li>PEX Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li><li>PB Oxygen Barrier Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen barrier pipes market includes three main types: PE-RT (Polyethylene Raised Temperature), PEX (Cross-Linked Polyethylene), and PB (Polybutylene). PE-RT pipes offer flexibility and resistance to high temperatures, making them suitable for heating systems. PEX pipes, known for their durability and resistance to corrosion, are popular in plumbing and radiant heating applications. PB pipes provide excellent impact resistance and are often used in hot and cold water systems. Each type caters to specific performance and installation needs in various applications.</p></p>
