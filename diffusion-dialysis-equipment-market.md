<p><ul><li>Acid Diffusion Dialysis</li><li>Caustic Diffusion Dialysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diffusion dialysis equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: acid diffusion dialysis and caustic diffusion dialysis. Acid diffusion dialysis is used to recover strong acids from waste streams, facilitating their reuse and minimizing environmental impact. Conversely, caustic diffusion dialysis targets the recovery of strong bases, improving process efficiency and reducing wastewater. Both types employ semi-permeable membranes to selectively allow ions to pass through, making them key technologies in industrial wastewater treatment and chemical recovery processes.</p></p>
