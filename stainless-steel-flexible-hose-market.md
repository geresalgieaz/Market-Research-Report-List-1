<p><p>The stainless steel flexible hose market is primarily categorized into strip-wound hoses and corrugated hoses. Strip-wound hoses feature a spiral design that provides high durability and resistance to kink, making them suitable for industrial applications requiring robust flexibility. Corrugated hoses, on the other hand, have a series of grooves that enhance flexibility and allow for superior pressure handling, often used in plumbing, chemical transport, and machinery where movement is essential. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, emphasizing flexibility and durability.</p></p>
