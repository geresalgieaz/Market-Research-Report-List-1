<p><ul><li>Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS)</li><li>Small Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The in-building cellular system market comprises primarily two types: Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) and Small Cells. DAS involves a network of antennas distributed throughout a building to enhance cellular coverage and capacity, making it ideal for large venues. Small Cells, on the other hand, are compact, low-powered base stations that provide localized coverage, suitable for areas with high user density. Both technologies aim to improve indoor connectivity and meet the growing demands for mobile data and voice services.</p></p>
