<p><p>The optical refractometers market is primarily divided into two types: traditional handheld refractometers and digital handheld refractometers. Traditional handheld refractometers use a prism and a light source to measure refractive index through manual observation, often requiring calibration and interpretation by the user. In contrast, digital handheld refractometers provide automated readings and enhanced accuracy through electronic sensors and digital displays, making them user-friendly and efficient for various applications. Both types serve industries like food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals for quality control and analysis.</p></p>
