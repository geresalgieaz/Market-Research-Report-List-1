<p><ul><li>PGNAA Technology</li><li>PFTNA Technology</li><li>Sodern Neutron Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cross-belt analyzers utilize various technologies for real-time material characterization in various industries. PGNAA (Prompt Gamma Neutron Activation Analysis) measures elemental composition by detecting gamma rays emitted from materials. PFTNA (Prompt Ferrous Titanium Neutron Analysis) identifies and quantifies materials based on differential neutron responses. Sodern Neutron Technology employs neutron interactions for precise analysis of material composition. Each technology serves distinct applications in mining, cement, and recycling, offering efficient, non-destructive analysis to optimize processes and enhance quality control.</p></p>
