<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Heart Rate Band Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency (5.3 Khz)</li><li>High Frequency (2.4 Ghz)</li><li>Ant Heart Rate Band</li><li>Bluetooth Heart Rate Band</li><li>Coded Transmission CODED</li><li>Uncoded Transmission CODED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart rate band market encompasses various types based on frequency and transmission methods. Low frequency (5.3 kHz) bands typically offer longer range but limited data rate, ideal for basic monitoring. High frequency (2.4 GHz) bands provide faster data transmission, suitable for real-time tracking and compatibility with various devices. Ant heart rate bands utilize ANT protocol for energy-efficient, low-latency communication. Bluetooth heart rate bands enable seamless connectivity with smartphones and apps. Coded transmission enhances security and data integrity, while uncoded transmission offers simplicity but less protection.</p></p>
