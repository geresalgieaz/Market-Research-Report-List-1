<p><p>Commercial floor standing condensing boilers are categorized into three main types: oil, gas, and solid fuel boilers. Oil boilers utilize heating oil to produce hot water efficiently, making them suitable for areas with oil supply infrastructure. Gas boilers operate using natural gas or propane, known for their quick heat-up times and lower emissions. Solid fuel boilers burn materials like wood or coal, offering renewable energy options but may require more maintenance and space. Each type serves specific commercial heating needs and preferences.</p></p>
