<p><p>The Hall-Effect Current Sensor market can be categorized into two main types: Linear (Analog) Electroflu Detector and Threshold (Digital) Electroflu Detector. Linear detectors provide a continuous output proportional to the sensed current, allowing for precise measurements in various applications. In contrast, threshold detectors generate a digital output based on predefined current levels, ideal for operations requiring distinct on/off signals. These variations support diverse applications in industries such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation, enhancing efficiency and performance.</p></p>
