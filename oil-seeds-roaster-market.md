<p><p>The oil seeds roaster market is segmented into two primary types: batch and continuous. Batch roasters process specific amounts of seeds in distinct cycles, ideal for small to medium-scale operations, allowing for flexibility in flavor and quality control. In contrast, continuous roasters operate in a steady flow, optimizing efficiency for large-scale production, reducing processing time, and ensuring uniformity. Both methods cater to different production needs, with batch focusing on customization and continuous emphasizing high output.</p></p>
