<p><strong>The Belden Alternative Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unscreened Multicore</li><li>Screened Multicore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Belden alternative cables comprise two main market types: unscreened multicore and screened multicore cables. Unscreened multicore cables feature multiple conductors without additional shielding, making them suitable for applications with low electromagnetic interference (EMI). They are cost-effective and commonly used in less sensitive environments. In contrast, screened multicore cables include shielding to reduce EMI, enhancing signal integrity in demanding environments. This makes them ideal for industrial and professional applications where performance and reliability are critical components.</p></p>
