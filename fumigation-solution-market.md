<p><p>The fumigation solution market is divided into two main types: natural and artificial fumigation. Natural fumigation utilizes organic materials, such as plant oils or essential oils, to create a pest-repelling atmosphere, appealing to eco-conscious consumers. In contrast, artificial fumigation employs synthetic chemicals or gas mixtures like methyl bromide or phosgene to effectively eliminate pests in various settings. Both methods aim to ensure effective pest control, with natural options focusing on sustainability, while artificial solutions prioritize efficiency and rapid action against infestations.</p></p>
