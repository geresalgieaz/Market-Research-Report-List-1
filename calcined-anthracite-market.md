<p><ul><li>Electrically Calcined Anthracite</li><li>Gas Calcined Anthracite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Calcined Anthracite market is primarily divided into two types: Electrically Calcined Anthracite (ECA) and Gas Calcined Anthracite (GCA). ECA is produced using electrical processes, resulting in higher purity and improved conductivity, making it ideal for use in aluminum production and electrodes. In contrast, GCA is produced through gas processing, offering cost advantages and serving primarily in applications such as steel manufacturing and as a filler in various industries. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing material performance across different sectors.</p></p>
