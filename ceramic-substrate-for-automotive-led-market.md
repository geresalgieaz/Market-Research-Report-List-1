<p><strong>The Ceramic Substrate for Automotive LED Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DPC Ceramic Substrate</li><li>HTCC & LTCC Ceramic Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive LED market utilizes various ceramic substrates, primarily DPC, HTCC, and LTCC types. DPC (Direct-Plated Copper) substrates provide excellent thermal conductivity and are suitable for high-power applications. HTCC (High-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) substrates are utilized for their robust mechanical properties and can withstand extreme temperatures, making them ideal for critical automotive components. LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) substrates offer flexibility and are used in complex circuitry designs. Each type enhances performance, reliability, and efficiency in automotive LED applications.</p></p>
