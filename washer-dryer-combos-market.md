<p><p>Washer dryer combos come in two main market types: one-piece units and combined units. One-piece models integrate both washing and drying functions in a single appliance, saving space and providing convenience, ideal for smaller living spaces. Combined units, on the other hand, consist of separate washer and dryer machines that may be installed close together but function independently. This setup offers greater capacity and flexibility, catering to households with larger laundry needs while retaining efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
