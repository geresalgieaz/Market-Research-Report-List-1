<p><p>The pod detergent market is primarily divided into two types: non-biological and biological. Non-biological pods contain synthetic ingredients that effectively clean clothes without enzymes, making them suitable for sensitive skin. They are generally hypoallergenic and ideal for everyday laundry. </p><p>On the other hand, biological pods include enzymes that break down stains and dirt at a molecular level, providing a powerful cleaning action. These are especially effective for tough stains and can work effectively at lower temperatures, saving energy and resources.</p></p>
