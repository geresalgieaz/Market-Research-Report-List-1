<p><ul><li>Conventional Distal Compression Plates</li><li>Fixed Angle Locking Distal Compression Plates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distal Compression Plates market comprises two main types: Conventional Distal Compression Plates and Fixed Angle Locking Distal Compression Plates. Conventional plates are designed to provide stable fixation and compression at the fracture site, enabling optimal healing. In contrast, Fixed Angle Locking Plates feature a locking mechanism that enhances stability and allows for angular stability, reducing the risk of screw loosening. These advancements offer orthopedic surgeons versatile tools for effectively managing complex fractures and achieving better patient outcomes.</p></p>
