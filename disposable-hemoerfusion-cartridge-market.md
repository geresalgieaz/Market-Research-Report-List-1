<p><strong>The Disposable Hemoerfusion Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Charcoal Hemoperfusion</li><li>Certain Resins Hemoperfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable hemoperfusion cartridge market comprises two primary types: charcoal hemoperfusion and certain resins hemoperfusion. Charcoal hemoperfusion utilizes activated charcoal to adsorb toxins and metabolites from the blood, effectively reducing levels of harmful substances. In contrast, certain resins hemoperfusion employs specialized synthetic resins designed to selectively capture and remove specific toxins, enhancing therapeutic efficacy. Both types are crucial in treating conditions like drug overdose and toxin-related illnesses, offering vital support in critical care and emergency medicine settings.</p></p>
