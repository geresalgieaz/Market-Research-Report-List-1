<p><p>The expansive mortar market is categorized based on temperature conditions affecting rock and reinforced concrete applications. Type I is designed for use in warmer conditions (25℃-35℃), ensuring effective expansion and bonding in high-temperature settings. Type II caters to moderate climates (15℃-25℃), balancing performance and curing time. Type III is formulated for colder environments (0℃-15℃), ensuring proper expansion and adhesion even in lower temperatures. These categories allow for optimized performance of expansive mortar across diverse construction scenarios.</p></p>
