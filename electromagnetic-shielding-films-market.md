<p><p>The electromagnetic shielding films market comprises three primary types: Conductive Adhesive Type, Metal Alloy Type, and Metal Microneedle Type. Conductive Adhesive Type films utilize conductive adhesives to create effective barriers against electromagnetic interference while ensuring easy application. Metal Alloy Type films are composed of thin layers of metal alloys, enhancing durability and shielding performance. Metal Microneedle Type films feature microscopic metal structures that improve signal attenuation, offering advanced shielding while maintaining flexibility. Each type caters to specific applications across various industries.</p></p>
