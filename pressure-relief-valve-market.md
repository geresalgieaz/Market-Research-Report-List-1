<p><p>The pressure relief valve market primarily features two main types: spring type and lever type. Spring type valves rely on a spring mechanism to maintain a set pressure level, releasing excess pressure automatically. They are widely used in various industries due to their reliability and simplicity. Lever type valves, on the other hand, utilize a lever mechanism for pressure adjustment, offering manual control. Both types play crucial roles in safeguarding systems from overpressure, ensuring operational safety across diverse applications.</p></p>
