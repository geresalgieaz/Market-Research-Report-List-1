<p><p>The mobile telescopic cranes market is primarily divided into two types: wheeled telescopic cranes and crawler telescopic cranes. Wheeled telescopic cranes feature a set of wheels for mobility, allowing them to navigate on paved surfaces and urban environments efficiently. Conversely, crawler telescopic cranes are equipped with tracks, providing superior stability and the ability to traverse rough terrains, making them ideal for construction sites with challenging ground conditions. Both types are versatile, enabling lifting operations in various industrial applications.</p></p>
