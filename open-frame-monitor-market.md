<p><ul><li>With Touch Screen (Resistive or Capacitive)</li><li>Without Touch Screen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Open Frame Monitor market is divided into two main types: with touch screen and without touch screen. Monitors with touch screens can be further categorized into resistive and capacitive types. Resistive touch screens respond to pressure, making them suitable for environments requiring durability and use with gloves. Capacitive screens, on the other hand, provide a more responsive experience and support multitouch gestures, ideal for modern applications. Monitors without touch screens focus on display quality and are typically used in environments where user interaction is minimal.</p></p>
