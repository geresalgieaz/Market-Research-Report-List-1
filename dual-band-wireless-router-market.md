<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dual Band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual-band wireless router market is categorized by several Wi-Fi standards, each offering different capabilities. 802.11a operates in the 5 GHz band, providing faster speeds but limited range. 802.11b, using the 2.4 GHz band, offers better range with slower speeds. 802.11g combines both, enhancing speed while maintaining range. 802.11n supports multiple channels for even higher throughput and improved range. Finally, 802.11ac, designed for the 5 GHz band, delivers significantly faster speeds and better performance for modern devices, catering to high-demand applications.</p></p>
