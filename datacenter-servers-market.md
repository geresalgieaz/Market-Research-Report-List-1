<p><ul><li>Tower Server</li><li>Rack Server</li><li>Blade Server</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The datacenter servers market comprises three main types: tower servers, rack servers, and blade servers. Tower servers resemble traditional PCs, offering standalone units ideal for small businesses needing expandability. Rack servers are designed for space efficiency, mounted in racks, suitable for larger operations requiring high-density computing. Blade servers take this a step further, housing multiple server blades in a single chassis, optimizing space and power usage, making them ideal for large datacenters seeking scalability and efficient management.</p></p>
