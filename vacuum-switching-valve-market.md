<p><p>The vacuum switching valve market comprises several types tailored for specific applications. Butterfly valves provide quick shut-off with minimal pressure drop, while gate valves offer low resistance in fully open or closed positions. Isolation valves ensure complete shut-off, and throttling valves regulate flow. Slit or door valves allow for precise sampling, while angle or inline valves direct flow at various angles. All-metal valves enhance durability, and pendulum valves leverage gravitational effects for control. Other types include specialized or hybrid designs for niche applications.</p></p>
