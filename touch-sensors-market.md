<p><strong>The Touch Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive</li><li>Capacitive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch sensor market comprises primarily two types: resistive and capacitive. Resistive touch sensors work by registering pressure applied to the surface, making them versatile for various environments, including those with gloves or styluses. Capacitive touch sensors, on the other hand, detect changes in electrical charge when a conductive object, such as a finger, touches the surface. They are commonly used in smartphones and tablets due to their sensitivity and accuracy, enabling multi-touch functionality and enhanced user experiences.</p></p>
