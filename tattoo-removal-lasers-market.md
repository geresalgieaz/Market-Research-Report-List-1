<p><p>The tattoo removal lasers market comprises three main types: Q-Switched lasers, Picosecond lasers, and Combination lasers. Q-Switched lasers use short pulses of light to break down ink particles, making them effective for a variety of ink colors. Picosecond lasers employ ultra-short pulses, enhancing precision and reducing treatment time, often resulting in less skin damage. Combination lasers integrate both Q-Switched and Picosecond technologies, offering customizable treatment options to optimize results and address different ink types and skin conditions.</p></p>
