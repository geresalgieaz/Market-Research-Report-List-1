<p><p>The Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment market is segmented into 2D and 3D types. 2D AOI focuses on surface inspections by capturing two-dimensional images to identify defects like misalignments and soldering issues. In contrast, 3D AOI utilizes advanced imaging techniques to provide depth information, allowing for a more precise evaluation of component height, solder volume, and other critical characteristics. This differentiation enhances quality control in manufacturing processes, particularly in the electronics industry, improving overall product reliability.</p></p>
